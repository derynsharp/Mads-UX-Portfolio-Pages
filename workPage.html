UX/UI DESIGN CASE STUDIES

Crescendo Podcasting App

Creating a mobile application designed to make launching and monetizing your podcast easier, from conceptualization to prototype.

My main role in this project was to conduct user interviews, conceptualize features from user insights and create the branding.

Dreaming Zebra Website Redesign

Improving the aesthetic and overall user experience of The Dreaming Zebra Foundation website.

My main role was restructuring the website, creating the branding and style guide and crafting wireframes for one of the most important call-to-actions: requesting art supplies.

Travelier Mobile App

Creating a mobile travel application that gets you to your most authentic travel experiences in record time.

For this project, I only worked with a team during the user research portion. From there on, I did conceptualization of features, branding, wireframing and building a tested, functional prototype on my own.
